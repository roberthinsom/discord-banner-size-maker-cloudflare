Understanding the Ideal Discord Banner Size

Before using the Discord Banner Size Maker, it’s essential to know the recommended banner size to ensure the design looks sharp and professional. Discord's official guidelines for a profile banner image are:

  • Size: 960px by 540px
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • File Size: Max 10MB
  • File Types: JPG, PNG, GIF (for animated banners)

While the image will look great on desktop versions of Discord, be mindful that mobile users will view your banner in a scaled format. Keeping the essential elements centered in the design will ensure it looks good on both platforms.

Tips for Creating Standout Discord Banners

To make your Discord banner truly pop and leave a lasting impression, consider these tips:

1. Consistency is Key

Make sure your banner aligns with the rest of your branding, such as your profile picture, server theme, and personal style. Consistent branding across all platforms increases recognition and helps build a cohesive digital identity.

2. Use High-Quality Images

Low-quality or pixelated images can make your banner look unprofessional. Opt for high-resolution images and vector graphics to ensure your banner looks sharp on all screens.

3. Add Subtle Animation

If you're looking to make your banner even more engaging, consider using subtle animations. A GIF banner can be a great way to showcase your creativity, but make sure it doesn’t overwhelm the viewer or distract from the core message.

4. Keep It Mobile-Friendly

As mentioned earlier, Discord users view banners differently on mobile devices. Make sure the key elements of your design are centered and won’t be cropped when viewed on smaller screens.

5. Test Before You Finalize

Before settling on your design, test it on both desktop and mobile Discord. This will give you a clear idea of how the banner looks on different devices and help you make any necessary adjustments.
